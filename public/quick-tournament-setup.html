Step 1: Tournament Details

Swiss System: Players with similar scores are paired, no player meets the same opponent twice.
Double Swiss: Similar to Swiss but players may face each other twice (once with white, once with black).
Round Robin: Each participant plays against every other participant.
Recommended for Swiss: 5-7 rounds for 32 players
